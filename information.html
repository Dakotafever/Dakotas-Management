What Is Devil’s Roulette?

Devil’s Roulette is a VR party game that combines suspense, risk, and strategy. Players take turns spinning fate by pulling the trigger of a pistol loaded with random rounds — will you survive or will luck betray you?

How to Play

  1. Enter Limbo / Lobby — join up with other players, chat, and wait for matches.
  2. Start a Match — once enough players are ready, the round begins.
  3. Take Turns — each player gets a chance to pull the trigger.
  4. Trigger Pull — the gun is loaded randomly; if the chamber fires, you're eliminated.
  5. Gadgets & Items — each round, special items can change the odds (e.g. stun, dodge boosts).
  6. Last Survivor Wins — the last player still alive (or meets certain win conditions) wins the match.

Tips & Tricks

  • Stay calm — don’t rush. Sometimes waiting or observing others gives insight.
  • Watch player behavior — hesitation or confident body movement can hint at their risk.
  • Use gadgets wisely — don’t waste powerful items early; save them for crucial moments.
  • Bluff & mind games — feign confidence or fear to influence others’ choices.
  • Manage risk — sometimes it’s safer to pass or delay rather than force a trigger pull.
